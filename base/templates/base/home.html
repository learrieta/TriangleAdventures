The Triangle's hidden Treasures

Triangle Adventures is the most fun kind of business: Started as a passion, which turned into a hobby, which turned into a business.

We are a team who e-biked every weekend on the Mountains to Sea trail around the Triangle area. We kept saying how lucky we were to have such a gorgeous trail in our back yard, yet we wondered how many people live in the area that have never even been on the trail before.

A “E-Bike Guided Tour” can be an exhilarating and eco-friendly way to explore a city or scenic area

What is an E-Bike?

An e-bike (aka electric bicycle) is a bike that uses electricity to assist the rider in pedaling.
So kick back, and let our state of the art bikes do the work for you.

Tours

Scenery Tour

2 hours of smooth, scenic riding on hand-picked sections of the most beautiful scenery on NC Mountains to Sea Trail. Picturesque moments, some local history, exhilarating e-bike ride. Snack included.

Burgers, Brews, and History too

Are you a burgers and beer connoisseur? Try some delicious local hot spots on this fan-favorite 3 hour tour. Beer and lunch included, along with some local history and fun back-country riding.

Tacos, Taps and Local Facts(NEW!)

Oh you are more of a Tacos and Taps lover? We’ve got your covered with the most authentic Mexican food around on this must-experience 3 hour tour. Beer and lunch included, along with a different kind of local history and paved trail riding.

Aventon E-Bike Test Drives (FREE)!
