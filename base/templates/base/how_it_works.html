How it Works

  • Pick your preferred adventure. (Come back often because we add adventure packages regularly for holidays and events!)
  • Click on the calendar link, and select the date you want to go. Keep in mind, we don’t run adventures every day, so make sure it’s on an available day. Typically weekends only, for now.
  • Click Book Now.
  • Go to the payment screen, register, then check out. Select any optional extras you’d like to add to your experience.
  • That’s it! You will be emailed the starting point. Please show up 15 minutes early to your experience.
  • Any questions, please email us at: info@triangle-adventures.com
